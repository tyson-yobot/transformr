# TRANSFORMR — Soul Document

## What TRANSFORMR Is

TRANSFORMR is the world's first AI-powered total life transformation platform.
It is not a fitness app. It is not a habit tracker. It is a complete operating
system for human transformation — powered by Claude AI, built on seven pillars,
and designed to help anyone become the best version of themselves.

## Owner

Automate AI LLC — Tyson Lerfald

## Target User

Anyone pursuing transformation across any area of life:
- Athletes chasing peak performance
- Entrepreneurs building empires alongside their health
- Parents reclaiming energy and vitality
- Anyone who has tried everything and needs an intelligent coach

## The Seven Life Pillars

1. Body — weight, body composition, measurements, progress photos
2. Fitness — workouts, strength, endurance, form, mobility
3. Nutrition — meals, macros, supplements, meal prep, grocery planning
4. Business — revenue, milestones, customers, financial tracking
5. Habits — daily rituals, streaks, accountability, gamification
6. Mindset — journaling, affirmations, mood, focus, vision board
7. Relationships — partner features, community, accountability partners

## Subscription Tiers

- Free: Core logging (workouts, nutrition, weight, habits, goals), basic streaks, barcode scanner, limited AI (5 meal scans/month, 10 chat messages/day)
- Pro ($9.99/month): Unlimited AI features, stake goals, vision board, goal cinema, dashboard builder, wearable integrations, smart notifications, lab scanner
- Elite ($14.99/month): Advanced AI analysis (form check video, progress photo analysis, detailed readiness), business & finance tracking, body-business correlation
- Partners ($19.99/month): All Elite features + partner linking, live sync workouts, joint streaks, partner challenges, shared vision board, couples weekly review

## AI Features

TRANSFORMR runs on Claude (claude-sonnet-4-20250514) via Supabase Edge Functions.
Every AI call receives the user's complete context: goals, last 7 days of data,
streaks, preferences, and coaching tone setting.

AI Vision Features (17):
- Form Check, Meal Camera, Labs Analysis, Supplement Scanner, Progress Photos
- Menu Scanner, Meal Prep Planner, Grocery List Generator, Sleep Optimizer
- Trajectory Simulator, Health ROI Calculator, Workout Narrator, Posture Analysis
- Correlation Insights, Adaptive Program, Challenge Coach, Compliance Advisor

AI Coaching:
- Daily Briefing (personalized to yesterday's data)
- AI Chat Coach (FAB on every tab, remembers conversation history)
- Weekly Review (AI-generated with real numbers)
- Journal Prompts (unique, based on previous entries)
- Workout Narrator (real-time coaching cues during workout)
