Automate AI LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates the TRANSFORMR mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and share your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name, email address, and profile photo
  • Date of birth, gender, height, and weight
  • Account credentials and authentication data

Health & Fitness Data

  • Workout logs (exercises, sets, reps, weights, duration)
  • Nutrition data (meals, macronutrients, water intake, supplements)
  • Body measurements and weight history
  • Sleep logs, mood tracking, and readiness scores
  • Lab results and biomarker data
  • Progress photos and form check videos

Financial Data

  • Revenue, expenses, budgets, and net worth tracking data
  • Financial goals and projections

Behavioral Data

  • Habit tracking data, streaks, and challenge participation
  • Journal entries and reflections
  • Vision board items and goal data
  • AI coaching conversation history

Device & Technical Information

  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Crash reports and performance data
  • Anonymous usage analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

  • App Functionality: To provide core features including workout tracking, nutrition logging, habit tracking, and financial management
  • AI Personalization: To power personalized AI coaching, recommendations, and insights based on your data
  • Analytics: To understand usage patterns and improve the app experience
  • Account Management: To manage your subscription, authenticate your identity, and communicate important updates

3. How We Protect Your Information

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted via TLS (Transport Layer Security)
  • Encryption at Rest: Your data is encrypted at rest within our database infrastructure powered by Supabase
  • Row-Level Security: Database-level policies ensure users can only access their own data
  • Biometric Authentication: Optional biometric lock (Face ID / fingerprint) adds an additional layer of security to your account

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate TRANSFORMR:

  • Supabase: Database hosting, authentication, and file storage. Subject to Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic (Claude AI): Powers AI coaching and personalization features. Conversations are processed to generate responses but are not used to train Anthropic's models
  • Stripe: Payment processing for subscriptions. We do not store your full credit card information

None of these third-party services receive your health data for their own purposes. Data shared with third parties is limited to what is necessary to provide the service.

5. Data Retention

  • Your data is stored for as long as your account remains active
  • Upon account deletion, all personal data is permanently deleted within 90 days
  • Anonymized subscription payment records may be retained for legal and accounting compliance
  • You may request data deletion at any time (see Delete Your Account)

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Export: Download your data in a portable format
  • Delete: Permanently delete your account and all associated data
  • Correct: Update or correct inaccurate personal information

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@automateai.com.

7. Children's Privacy

TRANSFORMR is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will promptly delete that information. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us at support@automateai.com.

8. Changes to This Policy
