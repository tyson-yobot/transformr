TRANSFORMR Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

AutomateAI ("we", "us", "our") operates the TRANSFORMR mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Data We Collect

Account & Profile Data

  • Name, email address, date of birth, gender
  • Profile photo and display preferences
  • Authentication credentials (managed via Supabase Auth)

Fitness & Health Data

  • Workout logs (exercises, sets, reps, weights, duration)
  • Nutrition logs (meals, calories, macronutrients)
  • Body measurements (weight, body fat %, circumferences)
  • Sleep duration and quality data
  • Mood and energy self-assessments

Media

  • Meal photos for AI-assisted nutrition analysis
  • Progress photos for body composition tracking
  • Form-check videos for exercise technique analysis

Location Data

  • Geofence-based location triggers (e.g., gym arrival/departure) used to automate workout tracking. We do not continuously track your location.

Business & Finance Data

  • Business metrics, revenue tracking, and financial goals you choose to log within the app's business tracking features.
  • Payment information is processed by Stripe and is never stored on our servers.

Device & Technical Data

  • NFC tag identifiers (used for equipment and location tagging)
  • Device type, OS version, app version, crash logs

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Core functionality: Tracking workouts, nutrition, measurements, and progress over time.
  • AI-powered insights: Generating personalized recommendations, analyzing meal photos, reviewing exercise form, and providing coaching feedback.
  • Automation: Triggering workflows based on geofence events and NFC tag scans.
  • Personalization: Tailoring content, plans, and the user experience to your goals.
  • Service improvement: Aggregated, de-identified analytics to improve app features.
  • Communications: Sending transactional emails and, with your consent, promotional updates.

3. AI Processing

TRANSFORMR uses Claude AI, provided by Anthropic, to power features such as meal photo analysis, workout plan generation, form-check feedback, and coaching conversations. When you use these features:

  • Relevant data (e.g., photos, logs, measurements) is sent to Anthropic's API for processing.
  • Anthropic processes data in accordance with their privacy policy and does not use your data to train their models when accessed via their API.
  • AI outputs are informational only and do not constitute medical, nutritional, or financial advice.

4. Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase — Backend infrastructure, database, authentication, and file storage.
  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — AI analysis and natural-language features.
  • Stripe — Payment processing for subscriptions. We do not store card details.
  • Spotify — Optional music integration during workouts. We access only playback controls; we do not store your Spotify credentials or listening history.

5. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data is retained for as long as your account is active.
  • Deleted accounts: Upon account deletion, personal data is removed within 30 days. Aggregated, de-identified data may be retained for analytics.
  • Media files: Photos and videos are deleted when you remove them or delete your account.
  • Backups: Encrypted backups are purged within 90 days of account deletion.

6. Data Security

  • All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256).
  • Authentication is managed through Supabase Auth with support for multi-factor authentication.
  • Row-level security policies ensure users can only access their own data.
  • Media files are stored in private, access-controlled storage buckets.
  • We conduct regular security reviews and dependency audits.

7. Your Rights

All Users

  • Access, export, or delete your data at any time from the app settings.
  • Withdraw consent for optional data processing (e.g., location, Spotify).
  • Opt out of promotional communications.

GDPR (EEA/UK Residents)

You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, data portability, and object to processing of your personal data. Our lawful bases for processing include consent, contract performance, and legitimate interests. To exercise your rights, contact us at the address below.

CCPA (California Residents)

You have the right to know what personal information we collect and why, request deletion, and opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell your personal information.

8. Children's Privacy

TRANSFORMR is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly. Users between 13 and 18 must have parental or guardian consent.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via in-app notification or email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
